服务器游玩规则

一、基本规定
1. 本规定依照服务器管理员的意愿，以团结互助共同发展为基本原则，以游戏默认规则为基础，以法律法规和公序良俗作为最低底线，为营造一个良好的游戏环境而编写。
2. 本规定中的“玩家”指注册时的用户名，“游戏”指该服务器的所有功能，“原则”指第一条中所指原则。
其他名词按照游戏中的自带解释进行解释。
3. 玩家不得以任何方式尝试干扰服务器正常工作。若有上述行为，服务器管理员保留追究法律责任的权利。
出现危害性特别严重的行为时，包括但不限于DDoS攻击、恶意提升权限、修改或窃取后台数据等行为，服务器管理员有可能也有必要将证据移交至公安机关处理。
4. 玩家的所有行为后果都应由自己承担。服务器仅提供平台并对玩家行为进行约束，但不对玩家行为后果负责。玩家使用服务器则将自动认为主动受本规定约束其行为。
5. 本规定与中华人民共和国的法律法规存在冲突时，以中华人民共和国的法律法规为准。
6. 中英版本规定出现翻译误差时，以中文版本为准。
7. 本规定最终解释权归服务器管理员所有。
二、一般规定
8. 不得与其他玩家相互攻击。
9. 不得与其他玩家相互对骂。
10. 不得与其他玩家恶意竞争。
11. 不得与其他玩家恶意结盟。
12. 不得窃取他人物资。
13. 不得干扰他人正常获取物资。
14. 不得干扰他人正常建筑。
15. 不得干扰他人正常与其他玩家进行互动。
16. 不得恶意使用指令。
17. 不得利用游戏本身漏洞，但是特性除外。
18. 不得长时间占用公共空间而不利用。
19. 不得随便使用其他玩家的装备及设施。
20. 不得通过任何行为影响游戏整体生态。
21. 不得作出与团结的行为。
22. 不得做出违反公共利益的行为。
23. 若出现上述行为，服务器管理员将会禁止该玩家短期或长期登录，甚至会永久封禁。
除上述惩罚外，视情节轻重，还可能追究其他责任。
24. 如果出现上述行为，其他玩家如实举报，服务器管理员应该进行奖励。
25. 任何玩家都有权利向服务器管理员提出建议和意见。
三、特殊规定
27. 如果玩家做出违反上述规定的操作，但并不是人为操作，应该免于处罚。
28. 如果其他个人恶意操控玩家，做出违反上述规定的操作，应该免于处罚。
29. 如果玩家做出违反上述规定的操作，但有合理理由，应该免于处罚。
四、相关权利说明
服务器属于服务器管理员的财产，玩家数据属于玩家个人所有。
该网站属于YouthFire所有。

Basic provisions
1. This regulation is prepared in accordance with the wishes of the server administrator, based on the basic principle of solidarity, mutual assistance and common development, based on the default rules of the game, and taking laws, regulations and public order and good customs as the minimum bottom line, in order to create a good game environment.
2. "Player" in these Provisions refers to the user name at the time of registration, "game" refers to all functions of the server, and "principle" refers to the principle referred to in Article 1.
Other nouns are explained according to their own explanations in the game.
3. Players shall not attempt to interfere with the normal operation of the server in any way. In case of any of the above acts, the server administrator reserves the right to investigate legal liability.
In case of particularly serious harmful behaviors, including but not limited to DDoS attacks, malicious elevation of permissions, modification or theft of background data, the server administrator may and must hand over the evidence to the public security organ for processing.
4. Players should bear all the consequences of their actions. The server only provides the platform and restricts the player's behavior, but is not responsible for the consequences of the player's behavior.
5. In case of any conflict between these provisions and the laws and regulations of the people's Republic of China, the laws and regulations of the people's Republic of China shall prevail.
6. In case of translation errors in the Chinese and English versions, the Chinese version shall prevail.
7. The server administrator has the final right to interpret this provision.
General provisions
8. Do not attack with other players.
9. Don't scold each other with other players.
10. Do not compete maliciously with other players.
11. No malicious alliance with other players.
12. Do not steal other people's materials.
13. Do not interfere with other people's normal acquisition of materials.
14. Do not interfere with other people's normal buildings.
15. Do not interfere with others' normal interaction with other players.
16. Do not use instructions maliciously.
17. The game itself shall not be exploited, except for features.
18. Do not occupy public space for a long time without using it.
19. Do not use the equipment and facilities of other players.
20. The overall ecology of the game shall not be affected by any behavior.
21. No act of solidarity.
22. Do not act against the public interest.
23. If the above behavior occurs, the server administrator will prohibit the player from logging in for a short or long time, or even permanently.
In addition to the above punishment, other responsibilities may be investigated depending on the seriousness of the circumstances.
24. If the above behaviors are reported truthfully by other players, the server administrator should reward them.
25. Any player has the right to make suggestions and comments to the server administrator.
Special provisions
27. If the player makes an operation in violation of the above provisions, but it is not a human operation, he shall be exempted from punishment.
28. If other individuals maliciously manipulate the player and make operations in violation of the above provisions, they shall be exempted from punishment.
29. If the player violates the above provisions, but has reasonable reasons, he shall be exempted from punishment.
